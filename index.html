I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at the University of Cincinnati Carl H. Lindner College of Business.

My primary research interests are macroeconomics, finance, and macro labor. Currently, my research focuses on understanding the long-run implications of financial frictions for U.S. firm dynamics.

Previously, I was a visiting scholar at the University of Chicago Becker Friedman Institute. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Texas at Austin in May 2023.
